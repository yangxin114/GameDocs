攻击威能概述 · Offense Temper Overview 攻击威能主要强化魔石（Brimstone）熔岩机制、深渊（Abyss）技能的影形叠层伤害、恶魔形态（Demonform）的群体加成，以及与易伤的联动控制链。这些词条是构建高爆发DPS术士BD的核心淬火选择。
Legendary Two-Handed Sword · 传奇双手剑
Demonblade of Lava（熔岩恶魔剑）
LEGENDARY
⚔️ 攻击威能 · Offense Temper
基础属性 · Base Stats
298 DPS +56 Willpower +64 Max Life +16% Damage +48% Crit Dmg
关键词条 · Key Temper Affix
★
Brimstone 魔石爆炸形成熔岩池，在5秒内造成其260%的伤害。站在熔岩池中可获得 Volatility（爆发性）。 Brimstone explode into lava pools, dealing 260%[+] of their damage over 5 seconds. Standing in lava pools grants you Volatility.
💡 机制解析：该词条让魔石（Brimstone）技能产生持续燃烧的熔岩池，将单次爆炸伤害转化为持续5秒的DOT，同时触发爆发性（Volatility）增益——这是许多术士爆发循环的重要触发条件之一。260%的基础倍率意味着强力的伤害延伸，尤其适合与AOE覆盖类技能组合。
Mechanic note: This temper transforms Brimstone's burst into a sustained lava pool DoT, multiplying total damage via the 260% multiplier over 5s while granting Volatility — a key buff trigger for many Witch burst rotations.

Insidious Demonblade（阴险恶魔剑）

295 DPS +60 Willpower +56 Max Life +17% Damage +50% Crit Dmg

★
深渊（Abyss）技能每层暗影形态（Shadowform）叠层额外造成3.4%增伤。拥有隐身（Stealth）时此加成提升400%。 Your Abyss Skills deal 3.4% increased damage for each stack of Shadowform. This bonus is increased by 400% while you have Stealth.
💡 机制解析：这是一个叠层+状态触发的复合增伤词条。暗影形态（Shadowform）叠层每层提供3.4%增伤，配合隐身（Stealth）状态可将增益放大5倍（×400%）。对于以深渊技能为核心输出的术士BD，这个词条能提供相当可观的爆发伤害窗口期，关键在于如何维持隐身状态并堆叠足够的暗影形态层数。
Mechanic note: A compound stacking + state-gated affix. Each Shadowform stack adds 3.4% damage to Abyss Skills, and triggering Stealth multiplies this entire bonus ×5 (400% increase). Maximize Shadowform stacks before entering Stealth for peak burst damage.

💡 机制解析：这是一个典型的群体聚怪增伤词条，在恶魔形态（Demonform）激活时，周围每个近战敌人提供6.8%增伤，20个敌人时达上限136%增伤。非常适合配合聚怪型副召唤物（如地狱裂缝形成的怪物堆），在农本悍匪、地牢密室等密集场景中输出极为可观。注意：需维持Demonform状态才能生效。
Mechanic note: A classic density-scaling affix. While Demonform is active, each nearby enemy adds 6.8% damage, capping at 136% (20 enemies). Pairs excellently with area-denial or crowd-pulling summons in dense dungeon rooms or boss arenas.

★
被妖术（Hexed）的敌人被你施加易伤（Vulnerable）时，减速74%持续3秒；被你减速时，变为易伤状态持续3秒。 Hexed enemies are Slowed by 74% for 3 seconds when you make them Vulnerable or become Vulnerable for 3 seconds when you Slow them.
💡 机制解析：这是一个双向联动控制链词条：对妖术（Hexed）目标施加易伤 → 触发74%减速；对Hexed目标减速 → 自动施加易伤。两种触发路径形成正反馈循环，使易伤和减速能互相触发，极大提升控场稳定性与暴击增伤来源。配合术士的诅咒（Hex）技能体系，可持续维持易伤+减速双Debuff。
Mechanic note: A bidirectional crowd-control loop: Slowing a Hexed target applies Vulnerable; making them Vulnerable Slows them. Both paths trigger each other, creating a persistent Slow + Vulnerable feedback loop that greatly increases control uptime and crit damage access against Hexed targets.

DEFENSIVE TEMPERS
防御威能 · 生存与控制词条
Legendary Helm · 传奇头盔
Doom Casque of Fiendish Oppression（恶魔压迫末日头盔）
LEGENDARY
🛡️ 防御威能 · Defense Temper
基础属性 · Base Stats
675 Armor +30 Willpower +25 Max Life +43 Life/5s +1 Demonology Skills
关键词条 · Key Temper Affix
★
召唤大恶魔（Greater Demon）时，周围敌人被击倒（Knock Down）5秒。小恶魔（Lesser Demon）技能对被控制（Crowd Controlled）的敌人造成62%增伤。 Summoning a Greater Demon Knocks Down enemies around it for 5 seconds. Your Lesser Demon Skills deal 62% increased damage to Crowd Controlled enemies.
💡 机制解析：这个词条将大恶魔的召唤变成一个5秒范围击倒，同时赋予小恶魔技能针对被控目标62%的条件增伤。两个效果形成协同：召唤大恶魔制造范围CC → 小恶魔技能追打享受增伤加成。这使得术士的召唤体系同时具备控场与爆发双重价值，是防御威能中综合效益最高的词条之一。
Mechanic note: Transforms Greater Demon summoning into a 5-second AoE Knock Down, then rewards follow-up with Lesser Demon Skills dealing 62% bonus damage to CC'd targets. The synergy loop: summon → AoE CC → blast with Lesser Demon abilities for massive damage.

Mechanic note: A passive reactive defense — when any Hexed enemy tries to attack you, 15% chance they're Knocked Down for 3s instead. Effectively adds an interrupt chance to every incoming attack from Hexed targets, significantly reducing damage taken in dense melee encounters.

RESOURCE TEMPERS
资源威能 · 法力与冷却词条
Legendary Ring · 传奇戒指
Blood-Staunch Hoop of Authority（权威止血圆环）
LEGENDARY
🔮 资源威能 · Resource Temper
基础属性 · Base Stats
62 All Resist +29 Willpower +13 Life on Kill +8.3% Damage +2% Crit Chance
关键词条 · Key Temper Affix
★
获得+10 最大统治值（Maximum Dominance）。 Gain 10 Maximum Dominance.
💡 机制解析：统治值（Dominance）是术士的核心资源条，决定了多项强力技能和被动的触发门槛与强度。额外的+10最大统治值直接扩大了资源上限，允许更长时间维持技能循环峰值状态，以及更频繁地触发需要消耗统治值的爆发技能。这是资源管理型术士BD必须优先考虑的淬火词条。
Mechanic note: Dominance is the Witch's primary resource gauge, gating powerful skill triggers and passive bonuses. +10 Maximum Dominance expands the resource ceiling, enabling longer peak rotation uptime and more frequent burst skill access. A must-have temper for resource-intensive Witch builds.
Legendary Amulet · 传奇护符
Axolotl's Eye of Ritual Synthesis（蝾螈之眼·仪式聚合）
LEGENDARY
🔮 资源威能 · Resource Temper
基础属性 · Base Stats
82 All Resist +28 Willpower +8% Damage +25% Crit Dmg +15 All Resist
关键词条 · Key Temper Affix
★
施放地狱火（Hellfire）技能时，使神秘（Occult）技能获得63% 冷却缩减（CDR）持续3秒。施放深渊（Abyss）技能时，使神秘技能获得63% 资源消耗减少（RCR）持续3秒。 Casting Hellfire Skills grants Occult Skills 63% Cooldown Reduction for 3 seconds. Casting Abyss Skills grants Occult Skills 63% Resource Cost Reduction for 3 seconds.
💡 机制解析：这是一个双触发跨技能系资源词条。使用地狱火技能 → 为神秘技能提供3秒63% CDR（冷却缩减）；使用深渊技能 → 为神秘技能提供3秒63% RCR（资源消耗减少）。两条路径均以神秘技能为受益目标，意味着合理交替使用三个技能系，可使神秘技能在几乎永久享有高效冷却或近零资源消耗状态，是技能循环设计的枢纽装备。
Mechanic note: A dual-path cross-school resource affix. Hellfire usage → 63% CDR for Occult Skills (3s); Abyss usage → 63% RCR for Occult Skills (3s). Alternating between the three schools can keep Occult Skills in a near-permanent reduced-cost and reduced-cooldown state — a pivot piece for tight skill rotation builds.

Legendary Boots · 传奇靴子
Boneweave Treads of Impetus（骨织冲劲步行靴）
LEGENDARY
🌀 通用威能 · General Temper
基础属性 · Base Stats
338 Armor +29 Willpower +30 Max Life +8.9% Move Speed +17 All Resist
关键词条 · Key Temper Affix
★
靠近你的恶魔（Demons）时，移动速度提升18%。 You gain 18% increased Movement Speed while Close to your Demons.
💡 机制解析：这是一个召唤型移速词条，在靠近自身召唤的恶魔时获得18%移速加成。本身属性已含8.9%移速，合计效果下召唤流术士的机动性十分可观。恶魔型召唤物通常会追随玩家，因此该词条在大多数战斗场景中等效于无条件被动移速。配合通用属性，这双靴子在移动效率与全面属性之间取得良好平衡。
Mechanic note: Grants 18% movement speed while near your Demons — since demon summons typically follow you, this affix effectively functions as an unconditional passive move speed bonus during combat. Combined with the base 8.9% move speed roll, Witch summoner builds gain significant mobility from these boots.

术士专属独特装备说明 · Class-Exclusive Ancestral Unique Items 以下三件装备均为术士职业专属的先祖独特（Ancestral Unique）装备，需达到70级方可装备，且仅在"恨之领主（Lord of Hatred）"赛季内掉落。每件装备携带独特被动特效（Gold Text Affix），是术士核心BD架构的定义性装备。
Ancestral Unique Ring · 先祖独特戒指 · 术士专属
The Fecund Seal（丰饶封印）
ANCESTRAL UNIQUE
✨ 术士专属 · Witch Exclusive
基础属性 · Base Stats
86 All Resist +11% Willpower +8.5% Crit Chance 10% CDR +3 Sigil of Summons
独特特效 · Unique Power
★
召唤印记（Sigil of Summons）造成100%[x]增伤，并可在仪式外通过击杀额外召唤至多3只恶魔蜂群。 Sigil of Summons deals 100%[x] increased damage and you can summon up to 3 additional demon swarmers with kills outside the ritual.
💡 独特特效解析：丰饶封印将召唤印记（Sigil of Summons）提升为召唤核心技能。100%[x]增伤（乘算）等同于双倍伤害基数；更关键的是，通过仪式外击杀可额外召唤最多3只恶魔蜂群（Demon Swarmers），在清怪过程中持续扩大召唤物数量。这件戒指直接定义了"暗黑仪式召唤流"术士BD的玩法框架。
Unique power note: Transforms Sigil of Summons into a scaling summoner centerpiece. The 100%[x] multiplied damage effectively doubles the skill's base output, while the kill-triggered demon swarmer generation (up to 3 extra) creates a snowballing summon count during trash clearing — the defining ring for Ritual Summoner Witch builds.

Ancestral Unique Pants · 先祖独特裤子 · 术士专属
Kabraxis' Wall（卡布拉克希斯之墙）
ANCESTRAL UNIQUE
✨ 术士专属 · Witch Exclusive
基础属性 · Base Stats
2,004 Armor +121 Willpower +1,000 Max Life +505 Poison Resist +3 Wall of Agony
独特特效 · Unique Power
★
痛苦之墙（Wall of Agony）免费获得堕落军团变体（Fallen Army Variant），并造成30%[x]增伤。 Wall of Agony gains the Fallen Army Variant for free and deals 30%[x] increased damage.
💡 独特特效解析：卡布拉克希斯之墙强制为痛苦之墙解锁堕落军团变体（Fallen Army Variant），免去了原本需要投入技能变体点数的成本，同时附加30%[x]乘算增伤。这意味着该技能变体的部队型AOE效果可免费获得，释放出的技能点可重新分配到其他地方。对于以痛苦之墙为核心AOE清场技能的术士BD而言，这条裤子是几乎不可替代的毕业装备。
Unique power note: Grants the Fallen Army Variant of Wall of Agony for free — no skill point investment required — plus 30%[x] damage. This frees up skill points otherwise spent on the variant, while delivering the army-style AoE functionality. Near-mandatory endgame pants for Wall of Agony Witch builds.

Spine of Tathamet（塔萨美特之脊）
ANCESTRAL UNIQUE
✨ 术士专属 · Witch Exclusive
基础属性 · Base Stats
815 DPS +121 Willpower +300 Life on Kill +75% Damage +3 Hell Fracture
独特特效 · Unique Power
★
地狱裂缝（Hell Fracture）造成120%[x]增伤，且每第3次施放创造一次三重裂缝（Triple Fracture）。 Hell Fracture deals 120%[x] increased damage and every 3rd cast creates a triple fracture.
💡 独特特效解析：塔萨美特之脊彻底改变了地狱裂缝技能的输出节奏。120%[x]乘算增伤使每次裂缝伤害翻倍，而每第3次施放产生三重裂缝机制则在第3次施放时爆发出3倍裂缝覆盖面积，大幅提升AOE清场效率。这把武器的DPS（815）在术士武器中属于顶级，配合+3地狱裂缝等级加成，是地狱裂缝流术士的毕业武器。
Unique power note: Fundamentally reshapes Hell Fracture's rhythm. The 120%[x] multiplied damage doubles each fracture's output, while the triple fracture on every 3rd cast creates a massive AoE spike every few casts. At 815 DPS base with +3 Hell Fracture levels, this is the definitive endgame weapon for Hell Fracture Witch builds.

BUILD SYNERGY NOTES
搭配建议 · 核心协同思路
⚔️
召唤爆发流 · Summon Burst Build 丰饶封印（+召唤印记增伤+蜂群召唤）＋恶魔压迫头盔（大恶魔击倒→小恶魔追打62%增伤）构成召唤→CC→追打的完整爆发循环。建议搭配减少召唤冷却的神秘技能词条以维持召唤频率。
🌀
深渊隐身流 · Abyss Stealth Build 阴险恶魔剑（暗影形态叠层×隐身400%加成）＋蝾螈之眼护符（深渊技能→神秘技能RCR降低）形成高强度爆发窗口。堆叠暗影形态后进入隐身触发爆发，深渊技能同步降低神秘技能资源消耗。
🔮
地狱裂缝流 · Hell Fracture Build 塔萨美特之脊（120%增伤+第3次三重裂缝）＋蝾螈之眼（地狱火技能→神秘技能CDR缩减）打造高频三重裂缝循环。配合卡布拉克希斯之墙（痛苦之墙免费变体）可兼顾清怪与单体。
⚠️
术士专属独特注意事项 · Class-Unique Notes 三件术士专属独特装备（丰饶封印、卡布拉克希斯之墙、塔萨美特之脊）均需70级且仅限术士职业装备。农本赛季内从恨之领主（Lord of Hatred）BOSS掉落概率最高，也可通过大秘境超60层获取机会。
